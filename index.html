Lizzo

About Lizzo

Lizzo, born Melissa Viviane Jefferson, is an American singer, rapper, and songwriter. She was born in Detroit Michigan and is well known for her outgoing and lively personality as well as her empowering music. Lizzo has become a prominent figure in the music industry and is also well known for her promotion of body positivity.
